Welcome to my blog. This is where I will write about the process of building things — the ideas, the dead ends, and the moments where something finally clicks.

## Why blog?

I have always found that writing things down helps me think more clearly. A project is never really done until you can explain it to someone else.

## What to expect

- Project postmortems: what went right, what went wrong
- Technical notes on tools and techniques I find interesting
- Random experiments and side quests
